Datathon-Quartz

A Datathon, or hackathon for data, is a time-constrained event where people form teams to collaborate intensively on data-related projects. Datathons are a great way to inject creativity and fun into a data team's work, explore unusual ideas, and identify promising avenues for further work or investigation.

STEP 1

The required dataset will be provided to the participating teams along with the problem statement. All the teams have to understand the scenario and try to find the root cause of the given problem based on the given dataset.

STEP 2

The provided data should be cleaned, processed and also create reports in visualizated inorder to analyse the data more precisely. Team are free to use tools like Python, R, Tableau, PowerBI, Microsoft Excel, Google Studio, Hadoop, etc.

STEP 3

All the qualified teams should present the found their insights to the panel of judges. Presentation sholud be completely in English. After Presentation, Team can answer the questions raised by the juries.
